Chopta

45 min drive 4-5 hours

Known as the 'Mini Switzerland of India', Chopta offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and is the base for treks to Tungnath and Chandrashila.

Tungnath Temple

5 km trek 4-6 hours

The highest Shiva temple in the world and the highest of the five Panch Kedar temples. The trek offers stunning views of the Himalayas and passes through beautiful meadows.

28 km away

Chandrashila Peak

6 km trek 5-7 hours

Offers 360-degree views of the Himalayan range including Nanda Devi, Trishul, and Chaukhamba peaks. The summit is accessible via a trek from Tungnath temple.

Food & Dining

Savor the flavors of the Himalayas

Authentic Pahadi Cuisine

At TheHillyDays, we believe that food is an integral part of the travel experience. Our kitchen serves delicious, home-cooked meals prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Breakfast

Start your day with a hearty breakfast featuring local specialties like Aloo Paratha, Pahadi Poori, fresh fruits, and our special masala chai.

Lunch & Dinner

Enjoy traditional Pahadi thali with dishes like Kafuli, Chainsoo, Gahat Ki Dal, and Mandua Ki Roti. We also serve Indian and Continental cuisine.

Evening Tea

Unwind with a cup of locally-grown tea and snacks while enjoying the sunset over the mountains.
